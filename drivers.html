Our Drivers

At Hoock Trucking, we understand our drivers are our most important asset. Therefore, we have put together the best driver trainers and team that we are able. With over 40 years of combined experience, our driver trainers help teach our new team members what to expect at Hoock Trucking, and what is expected of them.

Driver Trainer

Interested in Joining Hoock Trucking?

At Hoock Trucking we are always looking for the best drivers in the Midwest. Working at Hoock Trucking requires a commitment to safety and punctuality, but rewards those that share these values. Hoock Trucking follows all FMCSA Regulations, and expects our drivers to know them as well.

Benefits of Working at Hoock Trucking

  • Sign on bonus based on experience.
  • 401K with company match.
  • Full medical, dental, and vision insurance after training.
  • Vacation and sick time that rolls over year to year.
  • Paid holidays.
  • Retention pay after 8 hours.

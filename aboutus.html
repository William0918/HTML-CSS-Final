About Us

Hoock Trucking, LLC was started in 2017 by William Hoock with the help of a few key people.

William Hoock

CEO & Founder

William started driving a truck in his 20's and saw many issues with the current system. In order to fix these issues, he took a leap and started Hoock Trucking. When he started, he wanted to bring the best team to the business. With a focus on family and experience, William found the best people to fill the roles within his company.

William@HoockTrucking.com

Jane Hoock

CFO And Human Resources

Sister of William, Jane is key in keeping Hoock Trucking running smoothly. Jane graduated from Indiana University with a master's degree in accounting. She had worked for logistics companies for the 5 years before joining her brother at Hoock Trucking.

Jane@HoockTrucking.com

John Smith

COO

John comes to us from a national trucking company that William worked at. John wanted the opportunity to work with William to create a trucking company that focused on driver satisfaction and customer service. John is the leader of our customer service team, as well as our dispatch team.

John@HoockTrucking.com
